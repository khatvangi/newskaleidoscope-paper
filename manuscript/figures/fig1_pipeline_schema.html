Figure 1. NewsKaleidoscope analysis pipeline. Tier 1-2 sources (institutional news) flow through text extraction, translation, and LLM-based framing analysis. Each article is analysed independently with no predefined categories. Emergent clusters form from semantic similarity between free-text framing descriptions. All LLM inference runs on local hardware (2×TITAN RTX + RTX A4000).
